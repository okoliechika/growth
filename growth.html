GOU/U21/CSC/723

I gave my all and all when I started in my early age, my energy my day and night I gave it all for the love of what I do best. In the year 1999 where I met a young copper whose name was Lily, she was tall and slim with brown eyes this lady taught me how to survive in the jungle meanwhile I was in 200 level computer science at university of nsukka Nigeria. The young lady Lily taught me so many things to do as an entrepreneur she is she taught me how to be a CEO of a company which I am today I took her class for a straight two years learning at the end of that training I was already in my finals (year 4). Then I told her that I could not continue as a CEO because of my exams and projects then she gave me reasons why I should be humble and courageous to continue the process of learning how to be a CEO then suddenly I shouted at her and told her that I'm not interested again then she smiled and apologized to me for teaching me at the first place. In the year 2002 I was done with uni then my mother sent me to Abuja for holiday I stayed in Abuja for two good years and finally came back during the Year 2004 24th October that was when I was told to come and prepare for nysc program I felt happy and I was so overjoyed without knowing I'm entering hell. It is already 3rd January 2005 at 6:00 p.m. I was on my way to IMo state where I will serve my country this is where the hell began the road of imo is so bad to the extent our bus had to stop on the road and we started looking for help around 8:00 p.m. to God be the Glory we reached imo around 10:00 p.m. what a day. As soon as I heard that ola was Lily's cousin I rushed out for her and give her a warm hug and gladly told her to please give me Lily's number that I would like to speak with her so ola said no problem I'll give you the number, after a while she came back, give me the number I called the lady and to my greatest surprise lily was in America with her two beautiful babies then I really thank her and told her all the news and everything and then she wowed and congratulated me for picking up her advice after school and that was how I became a CEO of jimka enterprise.
